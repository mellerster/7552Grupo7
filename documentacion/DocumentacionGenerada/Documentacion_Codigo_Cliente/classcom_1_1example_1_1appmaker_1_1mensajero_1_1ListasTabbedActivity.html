SectionsPagerAdapter com.example.appmaker.mensajero.ListasTabbedActivity.mSectionsPagerAdapter
package

The android.support.v4.view.PagerAdapter that will provide fragments for each of the sections. We use a FragmentPagerAdapter derivative, which will keep every loaded fragment in memory. If this becomes too memory intensive, it may be best to switch to a android.support.v4.app.FragmentStatePagerAdapter.
